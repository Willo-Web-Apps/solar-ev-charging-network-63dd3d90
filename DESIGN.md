# Design DNA — Solva

## Visual Personality: bold-energetic with organic-natural warmth
## Layout: startup
## Typography: elegant-serif meets modern-geometric

### Color Palette
- Primary (Amber Gold): #F5A623 → oklch(0.76 0.15 75)
- Deep Forest Green: #1A4D2E → oklch(0.32 0.10 148)
- Surface Light: #FDFAF4 — warm cream (not sterile white)
- Surface Dark / Text: oklch(0.18 0.02 148) — deep forest near-black
- Accent Pale Sun: oklch(0.96 0.04 85) — warm light wash

### Font Pairing
- Display: Fraunces — weight 700–900 — warm organic serif with personality (sun-soaked, confident)
- Body: DM Sans — weight 300–500 — modern, clean, perfectly readable

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Solar light hero**: Layered OKLCH radial gradients mimic morning sunlight — amber bloom from top-right corner, deep green base, grain texture overlay. The sun is always rising on Solva.
2. **Asymmetric features grid**: Large featured card spans 2 columns + two stacked smaller cards — visually echoes a solar panel array rather than a generic 3-col grid.
3. **Diagonal green-to-amber CTA**: Final CTA section uses a 135° gradient from deep forest green to warm amber — Colombia's landscape from forest floor to sunlit sky. No purple, no blue.
4. **Colombian warmth**: Spanish language section labels ("Cómo funciona", "Para propietarios") signal local authenticity to Bogotá/Medellín audience.
